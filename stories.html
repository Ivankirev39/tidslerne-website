Prof. Torben Bager

November 11, 2020

Prostate Cancer

On Constitution Day 2019, I received a shocking message from the hospital doctor: "You have aggressive prostate cancer and it has spread to the bones". A little later, he added: "You can live many more years, there are many new treatment methods on the way". On the same day, they gave me a double dose of anti-hormone injection.

patient story

Member

April 15, 2020

Visions for the Danish healthcare system

When I write this letter, it is in the hope of helping the many other elderly people who have found themselves in the same situation that I was in when I was 85 years old when I got cancer. The hospitals could only offer me a treatment whose effect was worse than the disease itself. It is difficult for old people to cope with the tough chemotherapy. It is inhumane and totally undignified for a country like Denmark that doctors do not want to open up to other mild human and life-prolonging methods, especially in light of the fact that the methods are widespread in our neighboring countries, such as Germany, Switzerland, England and Austria.

Qi Gong – let the life energy flow

We probably all know the feeling of being beside ourselves. The feeling of being out of balance, that it doesn't run like clockwork in oil. QiGong is one of the four cornerstones of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), along with acupuncture, herbal medicine, and TuiNa (a Chinese form of massage). QiGong can be translated as energy gymnastics or the art of cultivating our Qi, our life energy and vitality. They say that if you always follow Yin and Yang, you will never get sick.
