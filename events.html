Join our upcoming cancer support events and workshops

At Tidslerne, we offer a diverse range of events and workshops designed to support individuals affected by cancer. Our programs encompass self-help courses, educational lectures, creative workshops, and wellness activities, available both in-person and online via Zoom.

Who can join the events and courses?

Tidslerne welcomes everyone interested in integrative approaches to cancer support. Our community thrives on shared experiences, knowledge exchange, and mutual support. Membership is not a prerequisite for participation, but members enjoy additional benefits and discounts.
