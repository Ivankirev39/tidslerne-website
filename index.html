Who are the Thistles?

We are a voluntary, independent patient association founded in 1991, and we act as a bridge between conventional and complementary (alternative) cancer treatment. We are among the few associations that also provide knowledge about treatment abroad.

The association is primarily run by patient members and is independent of the pharmaceutical industry and doctors. Thistles arranges self-help courses and workshops for its members at a very limited out-of-pocket cost.

Support our mission

The thistle stands resilient â€“ sharp, beautiful, and unyielding. Like those facing cancer, it endures through the harshest conditions, a symbol of courage, healing, and hope.

Vision

Thistles works for a positive and open dialogue between therapist and patient, as well as for information about side effects and evidence for treatment options.

Mission

We support our members in choosing or not choosing treatment. We humans are individuals, and each patient must find their own path.
