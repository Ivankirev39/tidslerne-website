How do we charge?

The membership fee is DKK 225 annually as a patient member or as a support member. New members do not pay a membership fee for the year in question, but are usually charged a membership fee in the following year.

If you are a support or patient member, you can sign up for a subscription as a household member for just DKK 25 for others in the household
