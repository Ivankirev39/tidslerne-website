Why becoming a Tidslerne member matters

Becoming a member of Tidslerne means joining a community that understands the journey through cancer — whether you're a patient, survivor, or a loved one.

Why should you sign up?

We offer regular self-help workshops, webinars, and local events — both in person and online. Topics range from plant-based nutrition to mindfulness and holistic practices. These programs are crafted to support both your physical and emotional recovery.

By becoming a member, you’re also supporting a bigger mission: promoting patient rights, contributing to national health discussions, and encouraging broader awareness of integrative cancer care. Your voice matters — and with us, it’s heard.

Membership fees – What does it cost to join Tidslerne?

The membership fee is DKK 225 annually as a patient member or as a support member. New members do not pay a membership fee for the year in question, but are usually charged a membership fee in the following year. If you are a support or patient member, you can sign up for a subscription as a household member for just DKK 25 for others in the household
